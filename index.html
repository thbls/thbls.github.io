Meryenda ni Tiyang Amy

The "Meryenda ni Tiyang Amy" project seeks to preserve and promote Filipino culinary heritage by creating an accessible and engaging online resource. Through an interactive website, the project aims to reach a broad audience, sharing traditional recipes, cultural insights, and the joy of meryenda with food enthusiasts nationwide. This digital platform is designed to increase business range and sales of Filipino food culture.

Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
